Our Motivation

"MediAfford is designed to revolutionize the way individuals access and purchase medicines by offering a digital platform that not only tracks and displays a comprehensive catalog of pharmaceutical products but also emphasizes affordability and accessibility. The scope extends to managing user profiles, facilitating secure transactions, and promoting an informed decision-making process regarding drug purchases. The project will leverage technology to ensure that users can easily compare prices between generic and branded options, thus encouraging savings and wider access to necessary medications."
